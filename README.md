# USC Economics AI Working Group

This repository contains materials for the AI Working Group in the Department of Economics at the University of South Carolina. The group brings together faculty, researchers, and graduate students to learn about and share best practices for using AI tools to enhance research productivity.

## Purpose

Our goal is to collectively explore how AI tools can be integrated into academic economics research workflows — from literature review and data analysis to writing and coding. By sharing experiences and resources, we aim to help members of the department use these tools more effectively and responsibly.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Guides and tutorials** on using AI tools for common research tasks
- **Prompting strategies** tailored to economics research contexts
- **Curated resources** including papers, blog posts, and external references
- **Meeting notes and presentations** from working group sessions
- **Example workflows** demonstrating AI-assisted research techniques

## Getting Involved

Members of the USC Economics department are welcome to contribute. If you have resources, tips, or workflows to share, feel free to open a pull request or raise an issue.

## Contact

This working group is led by faculty in the Department of Economics at the University of South Carolina. For questions or to get involved, reach out through departmental channels.
